## Notable Canadian Contributions

- Canada approved the world's first Solana staking ETF (3iQ SOLQ), setting a precedent for regulated staking products
- Sol Strategies (HODL) is one of the largest publicly traded Solana-focused companies globally
- Figment is one of the largest institutional staking providers in the Solana ecosystem
- Superteam Canada is one of the most active Superteam chapters worldwide
